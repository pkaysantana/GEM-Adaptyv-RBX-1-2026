## Compliance

- Sequences: 25 (limit: 100)
- Length: 55–76 AA (limit: 250)
- Amino acids: standard 20 only
- Format: CSV, two columns (`Name`, `Sequence`)
- No duplicate names or sequences
- De novo designed: all backbones generated by RFdiffusion, sequences designed by ProteinMPNN. No motif scaffolding or lead optimisation from existing binders. Novelty relative to UniRef50 has not been independently verified by sequence search; designs are structurally novel by construction (diffusion-generated backbones).

## Design Pipeline

1. **RFdiffusion** — hotspot-conditioned backbone generation on RBX-1 (PDB 1LDJ), ~90 scaffolds
2. **ProteinMPNN** — sequence design at 3 temperatures (T=0.1, 0.2, 0.3), 5 samples each → ~1,500 candidates
3. **Biophysical filtering** — charge, hydrophobicity, repeats, length → 245 candidates
4. **ESMFold monomer screening** — predicted pLDDT > 75 → 59 candidates
5. **Boltz-2 complex screening** — pair_iptm_AB > 0.65 → 25 candidates (PASS)

## Ranking Rationale

Candidates were ranked by:
1. **pair_iptm_AB** (primary) — Boltz-2 interface predicted TM-score between binder and RBX-1. This is a model-based confidence metric for interface quality, not an experimental binding affinity.
2. **Monomer pLDDT** (secondary) — ESMFold confidence that the binder folds into the intended topology.
3. **Biophysical heuristics** (tertiary) — charge balance, hydrophobic content, absence of low-complexity regions.

All 25 submitted sequences passed every gate. The top-ranked design has pair_iptm_AB = 0.85; the lowest has 0.65.

**Important**: pair_iptm_AB and pLDDT are computational prioritisation metrics. They correlate with but do not guarantee experimental binding. Actual performance will be determined by wet-lab assays (SPR/BLI, expression, Tm).
